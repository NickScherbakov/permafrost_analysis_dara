## Methodology from PDF Source (Pages 1-11)

### Core LC-Curve Algorithm
1. **Normalization**: v_t = (z_t - z_min) / (z_max - z_min)
   - Min-max normalization to [0,1] range
   - Constant series (z_max = z_min) excluded

2. **LC-Curve Formula**: LC(t) = Σ_{i=t}^n v_i / Σ_{i=1}^n v_i
   - Ratio of remaining sum to total sum at each time point

3. **Diagonal (Uniformity Reference)**: D(t) = (n-t) / n
   - Linear decline from 1 (t=0) to ~0 (t=n)

4. **Deviation**: Δ(t) = LC(t) - D(t)
   - Positive Δ: effect concentrated early
   - Negative Δ: effect concentrated late

5. **Transformation**: Δ*(t) = (Δ(t) - Δ_min) / (Δ_max - Δ_min), then raised to 4th power
   - Enhancement of significant deviations
   - Suppression of small fluctuations

6. **Final LC-Curve**: Recalculated from transformed Δ* series
